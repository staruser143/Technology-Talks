| **Deploy AWS Outposts in the company data center. Launch EC2 on Outposts and create an S3 bucket on Outposts. | Use **Dedicated Hosts** for EC2 and use S3 in the Region                                                                                      | ❌ Distractor  | Dedicated Hosts give **single-tenant EC2** but still located in **AWS Regions**. When the question says **EC2 + S3**, **sub-second latency**, and **must be on company-controlled hardware**, the answer is almost always:
