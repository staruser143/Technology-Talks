Below is a **Broker / Agent–specific Service Rationalization Scoring Model**, tailored for **quote‑to‑enroll journeys**, **ACA / Small–Large Group**, **commissioning**, **licensing**, and **exchange integrations**.  
This variant reflects broker realities: **conversion, speed, compliance, ecosystem fit, and operational friction** matter more than pure IT cost.

***

## 1. Sales & Conversion Criticality (20%)

**Rationale:**  
Broker tools directly influence **sales velocity, drop‑offs, and close rates**.

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Quoting, plan comparison, eligibility, instant bind
*   **3** – Sales-adjacent support (docs, rate lookups)
*   **1** – Non-sales back-office utility

🔑 *High score → rarely retired; usually modernized or consolidated*

***

## 2. Regulatory & Licensing Compliance (15%)

**What matters**

*   State licensing checks
*   Commissions compliance
*   ACA exchange behavior
*   DOI auditability

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Required for compliant selling
*   **3** – Indirect compliance support
*   **1** – No regulatory relevance

⚠️ *Low score + high risk → Replace with compliant SaaS*

***

## 3. Broker / Agent Experience (BX / AX) (15%)

**Domain-specific driver**

*   Too many tools → broker abandonment
*   UX issues → shadow IT (Excel, email)

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Single-pane, intuitive, fast
*   **3** – Usable but fragmented
*   **1** – Friction-heavy / manual

✅ *Low score → Consolidate aggressively*

***

## 4. Channel & Ecosystem Integration (15%)

**Critical integrations**

*   Exchanges (FFE / State)
*   Quote vendors
*   CRM (Salesforce)
*   DocuSign / e-signature
*   Commission systems

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – API-first, real-time
*   **3** – Hybrid/manual touchpoints
*   **1** – Point-to-point / batch only

🔁 *Low score → Replace or replatform*

***

## 5. Operational Efficiency & Cycle Time (10%)

**Focus**

*   Quote turnaround time
*   Manual underwriting touches
*   Rework from errors

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Straight-through processing
*   **3** – Partial automation
*   **1** – Heavy manual handoffs

⏱️ *Low score = lost sales*

***

## 6. Data Sensitivity & Commission Risk (10%)

**Unique broker factors**

*   PII / PHI
*   Commission leakage
*   Chargebacks / disputes

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Sensitive data, high exposure
*   **3** – Mixed sensitivity
*   **1** – Minimal data risk

🔒 *Low maturity → Reduce scope or retire*

***

## 7. Technical Health & Agility (10%)

**Broker tech reality**

*   Vendor portals
*   Custom wrappers
*   Legacy rule engines

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – API‑driven, cloud-ready
*   **3** – Stable but slow
*   **1** – Rigid / EOL

🔧 *Poor health + high business value → Refactor*

***

## 8. Future Readiness (ACA, Individual, AI) (5%)

**Looks ahead**

*   Individual/ACA expansion
*   Quoting copilots
*   Agentic workflows

**Score Guidance**

*   **5** – Future-ready
*   **3** – Neutral
*   **1** – Blocks roadmap

🚀 *Low score → Replace before expansion*
